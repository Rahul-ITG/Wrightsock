The Double Layer Running Sock is the cornerstone of the Wrightsock collection. Using our patented double-layer construction, the inner and outer layers move together — eliminating the friction between your foot and sock that causes blisters.

Featuring a reinforced heel and toe, arch support band, and moisture-wicking CoolMesh® panels, this sock is engineered to perform from your first warm-up mile through the finish line.

Available in low, quarter, and crew heights to fit every shoe and preference. Machine washable and built to outlast the miles.

Patented Double Layer

Two sock layers move together — not against your skin — to eliminate blister-causing friction entirely.

CoolMesh® Ventilation

Strategic mesh panels on the top of foot channel air flow to keep feet cool and dry mile after mile.

Reinforced Heel & Toe

Extra-durable reinforcement in high-wear zones extends the life of the sock and adds cushioning where it matters most.

Arch Support Band

A snug elastic arch band holds the sock in place and provides added support to reduce fatigue on long runs.

Moisture Wicking

Advanced fibers pull moisture away from the skin and disperse it across the outer layer to keep feet dry.

Machine Washable

Durable construction holds up to regular machine washing. The double-layer bond won't separate, wash after wash.

  • Machine wash cold with like colours. Use a gentle cycle to preserve elasticity and double-layer bonding.

  • Do not use bleach or fabric softener. These break down moisture-wicking fibres and reduce performance over time.

  • Tumble dry low or air dry. Avoid high heat, which can damage elastic fibres and reduce the sock's lifespan.

  • Do not iron or dry-clean. For best results, wash inside-out to help maintain colour vibrancy over repeated washes.
