Anatomy of a Wrightsock

1

CoolMesh Ventilation

Strategic mesh panels across the top of the foot channel airflow to keep feet cool and dry. The open-weave construction allows heat to escape without compromising structural support.

2

Arch Support Band

A snug elastic band wraps the arch to hold the sock firmly in place and provide gentle compression. Reduces fatigue on long runs and prevents bunching mid-stride.

3

Double Layer Construction

The inner Dri-WRIGHT layer wicks moisture from skin while the outer layer provides cushioning and structure. Together they eliminate friction at its source.

4

Reinforced Heel

Extra-dense cushioning at the heel absorbs impact and extends durability in the highest-wear zone. Y-heel shaping keeps the pocket aligned for a slip-free fit.

5

Reinforced Toe Box

Seamless toe closure prevents irritation, while a reinforced knit in the toe box adds durability and protection during toe-off — when forces are at their peak.
